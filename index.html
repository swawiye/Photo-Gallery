The Concept of Eunoia

Eunoia is a Greek word meaning 'beautiful thinking'. It is the theme of this art showcase. We hope to help you achieve this by inviting you to slow down and let the imagery guide your thoughts somewhere calmer and softer through visual immersion.

This gallery is carefully developed to highlight certain aspects of life that make it beautiful, which are: Enchantment, Reflection and Golden Hour. They represent the vibrance and diversity of colour; depth and detail in different ways and forms; and the softness of the colour of sunlight(gold) respectively. These aspects are feautured as themes in each of the different sections.

Each collection is designed not just to be viewed, but to be felt. We hope you can enjoy the showcase!

Meet the Artist

Behind this showcase is a creative vision shaped by years of exploration of art through everyday life experiences. The master of this vision is Arketá Mátia.

She fully lives up to her name by inviting other people to see the world through her lens where they can perceive depth they have never felt before. Arketá carefully curated this gallery to show how light and shadow interact with form to create art. Each collection tells a cohesive visual story.
